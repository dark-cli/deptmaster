# Kaleem.dev Inspired Theme

## Color Analysis from kaleem.dev

Based on the website design, here are the key colors:

### Light Mode:
- **Primary Accent**: Deep orange/red (#E65F1E or #D35400) - Used for headings, CTAs, highlights
- **Background**: Clean off-white (#F9F9F9 or #FAFAFA)
- **Surface**: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- **Text Primary**: Dark charcoal (#333333 or #2B2B2B)
- **Text Secondary**: Medium gray (#777777)
- **Minimal color usage** - accent color really pops

### Dark Mode:
- **Primary Accent**: Lighter orange (#FF8147 or #F28C38) - For better contrast on dark
- **Background**: Near-black (#1C1C1C or #0F0F0F)
- **Surface**: Dark gray (#2A2A2A)
- **Text Primary**: Light gray/white (#EFEFEF or #F5F5F5)
- **Text Secondary**: Medium gray (#CCCCCC)

## Design Characteristics:
- Clean, minimal design
- Warm accent color stands out
- High contrast for readability
- Professional yet approachable
- Developer/portfolio aesthetic
