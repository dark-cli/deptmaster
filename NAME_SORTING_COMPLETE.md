## How It Works

- **Name (A to Z)**: Sorts transactions by contact name alphabetically (A to Z)
- **Name (Z to A)**: Sorts transactions by contact name reverse alphabetically (Z to A)
- "Unknown" contacts appear at the end for A to Z, or at the beginning for Z to A
